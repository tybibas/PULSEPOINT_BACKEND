# SOURCEPASS - Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

> Company: SOURCEPASS
> Website: https://www.sourcepass.com/
> Geographic Focus: National (US-based, with emphasis on Northeast/Mid-Atlantic)

---

## Company Overview

**Tagline:** "IT That Puts People First"

**What They Do:**
Sourcepass is a Managed IT Services Provider (MSP) offering a "Next Generation IT Experience" through strategic guidance, award-winning service, and proprietary innovation. They differentiate from traditional MSPs by focusing on people and digital experience, not just break-fix support.

**Core Services:**
1. **Managed IT Services** - 24x7 help desk, proactive monitoring, strategic IT roadmapping
2. **Cybersecurity & MDR** - Managed Detection and Response, SIEM, phishing protection, HIPAA/FINRA compliance
3. **Digital Transformation** - Cloud migration (Azure/AWS), Microsoft 365, Power Platform
4. **Staff Augmentation** - Specialized IT talent for manufacturers and retail distributors
5. **Quest® Platform** - Proprietary client dashboard for IT visibility and ticket management

**Key Differentiators:**
- "Local Roots, National Strength" - personalized care with national resources
- Quest® innovation platform for client visibility
- Strategic maturity focus (beyond break-fix)

## Primary Target Segments

### Segment 1: Healthcare Organizations (HIPAA-Regulated)
**Why:** Multiple success stories (Medical Society of Delaware, Delaware Breast Cancer Coalition). Deep expertise in HIPAA compliance, data security for sensitive patient information.

**Target Companies:**
- Regional hospitals and health systems
- Medical societies and associations
- Specialty clinics and care facilities
- Healthcare non-profits

**Target Titles:**
- Chief Information Officer (CIO)
- IT Director / VP of IT
- Director of Operations
- Chief Executive Officer (smaller orgs)
- Compliance Officer

---

### Segment 2: Manufacturing & Distribution
**Why:** Strong case studies (Spectronics, Centrix, Universal Traffic Service). Expertise in staff augmentation and global IT support for logistics and manufacturing.

**Target Companies:**
- Mid-market manufacturers ($20M-$500M revenue)
- Retail distributors with complex logistics
- Industrial equipment companies
- Food & beverage manufacturers

**Target Titles:**
- VP of Operations
- IT Director / Manager
- Plant Manager
- Chief Operating Officer
- Director of Supply Chain

---

### Segment 3: Professional Services (Insurance, Legal, Financial)

- Mid-size law firms
- Accounting firms
- Financial advisory firms

**Target Titles:**
- Managing Partner
- Director of IT / Technology

### Segment 4: Non-Profit & Social Services

## Trigger Event Affinity

Based on service offerings and past clients, the best trigger events for Sourcepass are:
1. **Compliance deadlines** (HIPAA audits, FINRA requirements)
2. **IT leadership changes** (new CIO/IT Director looking to modernize)
3. **Cybersecurity incidents** (breaches in similar companies)
4. **Growth events** (funding rounds, acquisitions requiring IT integration)
5. **Digital transformation initiatives** (cloud migration announcements)

---

## Disqualifiers

Avoid targeting:
- Very small businesses (<25 employees) - likely can't afford managed services
- Enterprise companies (>2000 employees) - typically have in-house IT
- Companies with recent MSP partnerships announced
- Retail/hospitality (not a strength area based on success stories)
