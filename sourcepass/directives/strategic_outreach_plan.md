### Healthcare Outreach Playbook

**Target Titles (in priority order):**
1. Chief Information Officer (CIO)
2. Chief Digital Information Officer (CDIO)
3. VP of IT / IT Director
4. Compliance Officer / Chief Compliance Officer
5. CEO (for smaller practices <100 employees)

**Target Company Profile:**
- Regional health systems (100-2,000 employees)
- Medical societies and associations
- FQHCs (Federally Qualified Health Centers)
- Specialty practices (fertility, GI, dental groups)
- Healthcare non-profits
