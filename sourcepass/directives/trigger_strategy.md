# SOURCEPASS - Trigger Event Strategy

> Trigger events that signal a company may be ready for managed IT services.

---

## Primary Trigger Events

### 1. IT Leadership Changes
**Signal:** New CIO, IT Director, or VP of Technology appointed

**Why it works:** New IT leaders often want to modernize, re-evaluate vendors, and make their mark. They're open to new partnerships and often inherit technical debt they want to address.

**What to search for:**
- "[Company] appoints new CIO"
- "[Company] hires IT director"
- LinkedIn job change alerts for IT leadership titles

**Priority:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Highest)

---

### 2. Cybersecurity Incidents (Industry-Wide)
**Signal:** Data breach or ransomware attack at a similar company in the same industry

**Why it works:** When a competitor or peer gets hit, executives start asking "could that happen to us?" Creates urgency for security assessments.

**What to search for:**
- "Healthcare data breach [region]"
- "Ransomware attack manufacturing"
- "HIPAA violation [state]"

**Priority:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Highest)

---

### 3. Compliance Deadlines & Audits
**Signal:** HIPAA audit announcements, FINRA compliance updates, new regulatory requirements

**Why it works:** Compliance creates hard deadlines. Companies scrambling to meet requirements are highly motivated to find expert partners.

**What to search for:**
- "HIPAA audit [company name]"
- "Compliance deadline healthcare 2026"
- "FINRA cybersecurity requirements"

**Priority:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (High)

---

### 4. Growth Events (Funding, Acquisition, Expansion)
**Signal:** Series A/B funding, acquisition announcement, new office/facility opening

**Why it works:** Growth strains existing IT infrastructure. Acquisitions require IT integration. Funding means budget for improvements.

**What to search for:**
- "[Industry] funding round"
- "[Company] acquires"
- "[Company] expands to new location"
- "Private equity [industry] investment"

**Priority:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (High)

---

### 5. Digital Transformation Announcements
**Signal:** Cloud migration projects, legacy system replacement, ERP implementations

**Why it works:** Major technology initiatives often reveal the need for additional support. Companies mid-project may realize they're overwhelmed.

**What to search for:**
- "[Company] cloud migration"
- "[Company] Microsoft 365 implementation"
- "[Industry] digital transformation"

**Priority:** ⭐⭐⭐ (Medium)

---

## Trigger Event Search Strategy by Industry

| Industry | Best Trigger Events |
|----------|---------------------|
| Healthcare | HIPAA audits, breaches at similar orgs, new CIO |
| Manufacturing | Expansion/new facilities, acquisitions, supply chain tech investments |
| Professional Services | Partner changes, compliance requirements, cyber incidents |
| Non-Profit | Grant funding for technology, leadership transitions, compliance |
